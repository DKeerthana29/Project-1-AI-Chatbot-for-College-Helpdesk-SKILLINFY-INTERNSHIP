Admission Requirements

  • A minimum aggregate of 60% in High School (10+2 pattern) or equivalent.
  • Valid scores in National/State Level Entrance Examinations (e.g. JEE, NEET, or EM-AT).
  • Original mark sheets and passing certificates must be submitted during counseling.
  • Proof of identity and updated passport-size photographs.
